Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

TourGo (“we,” “us”) operates a free property management platform that allows hotel owners and managers in Colombia to manage bookings, customers, transactions, and rooms.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data in compliance with Colombian law.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

Personal Data (Datos Personales)

We collect:

  • Full name, ID number (Cédula), email, phone, address
  • Business data: hotel name, NIT, chamber of commerce registration
  • Guest data: name, ID, contact info, payment method (encrypted)
  • Transaction records: booking dates, amounts in COP, invoices

Usage & Technical Data

Automatically collected:

  • IP address, browser, device type, operating system
  • Pages visited, session duration, clicks

Cookies

We use functional and analytical cookies. You can disable them in your browser, but some features may be limited.

2. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING

We process your data to:

  • Provide and operate the TourGo platform
  • Manage bookings, payments, and invoicing (DIAN compliance)
  • Send transactional emails and notifications
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations

3. DATA SHARING

We share data only with:

  • Cloud providers (e.g., AWS in São Paulo or Frankfurt) with SCCs
  • Payment gateways (e.g., PayU, Mercado Pago) under PCI-DSS
  • DIAN or judicial authorities when required by law
  • Successors in case of merger or sale

All recipients are bound by data processing agreements.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement:

  • TLS encryption in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • Access controls, firewalls, and regular audits

No method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER LEY 1581

You have the right to:

  • Know, update, and rectify your data
  • Request proof of authorization
  • File complaints with the SIC
  • Revoke consent or request deletion (when legally feasible)
  • Access your data free of charge once per month

Submit requests to: andresj.correas@outlook.com
We respond within 15 business days (Ley 1581, Art. 15).

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain data only as long as necessary or required by law (e.g., 5 years for tax records per DIAN). Inactive accounts are deleted after 3 years.

7. CHILDREN'S DATA

TourGo does not knowingly collect data from minors under 18. Any such data will be deleted immediately.

8. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Data may be processed in countries with adequate protection (per SIC list) or under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

9. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notice. Continued use implies acceptance.
