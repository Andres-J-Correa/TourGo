Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TourGo property management software.

What is TourGo?

TourGo is a comprehensive property management software designed to help property managers and landlords organize bookings, manage finances, track room cleaning, and gain valuable property insights all in one centralized platform.

Is TourGo free to use?

Yes, TourGo offers a free tier that allows you to get started with basic property management features. You can start using TourGo for free and upgrade as your needs grow.

What features does TourGo offer?

TourGo includes booking management, financial tracking and reporting, room cleaning status tracking, property analytics and insights, customer management, and invoice generation. All features are designed to streamline your property management workflow.

Do I need technical knowledge to use TourGo?

No, TourGo is designed with ease of use in mind. The intuitive interface makes it simple for property managers and landlords of all technical levels to manage their properties effectively.

Can I manage multiple properties with TourGo?

Yes, TourGo supports managing multiple properties from a single dashboard. You can organize bookings, finances, and insights for all your properties in one place.
